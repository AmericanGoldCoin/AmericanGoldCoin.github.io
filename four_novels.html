The Four Great Classic Novels

 This set of commemorative coin has 4 coins in total, each coin retailing for 10USD.


 The positive side depicts scenes from the following Chinese Great Literary Classics: A Journey to the West, Pilgrimage for Buddhist Scriptures; A Dream of Red Mansion, The Romance of West Chamber; Heroes of the Marshes, Wusong Fights the Tiger; and The Romance of the Three Kingdoms, Brothers in Peach Garden respectively. The back of the coins feature a relief of an American bald eagle. After minting, the coins are engraved for a high-relief effect and enameled in color for exceptional vibrancy, longevity, and beauty.


 These four novels, representing the greatest level of artistic achievement, are the immortal literary works in the history of Chinese culture. They are all best known for their meticulous depiction and profound ideological connotation. The "Four Great Classic Novels" special commemorative coin collection are legal investment commemorative coins, with 10,000 sets produced and distributed globally.
