G20 Summit Commemorative Coins

 The "G20 Member Countries" special commemorative coins collection was issued by the American Gold Coin Inc. to honor the first time the G20 international forum was hosted in Hangzhou, China. On this momentous occasion, China for the first time in history held the presidency of the G20.


 The complete set of 20 coins, valued at 20USD each, are 3.5cm in diameter. The front of the coins are engraved with the maps and representative animals of each of the 20 countries attending the G20 forum and finished in a high-relief color painting technique. The back is minted with the emblem of an American bald eagle, the symbol of the American Gold Coin Inc. This set is produced on a single limited-edition run of 10,000 worldwide.
