Chinese Zodiac Silver Coins

 On April 26, 2013, French businessman François-Henri Pinault, Chairman of Kering luxury group, announced his intention to return Chinese national treasures as a sign of goodwill.


 These treasures, which had been stolen and auctioned off overseas, consisted of bronze statue busts portraying the Chinese Zodiac Rat and Rabbit that originally stood in the Imperial Garden. American Gold Coin Inc. produced twelve silver coins featuring the Chinese Zodiac in the style of the YuanMingYuan Imperial Garden to celebrate this epoch-making historic event.


 The silver coins for "Chinese Zodiac of Yuanmingyuan Imperial Garden" weigh 12oz at 99.99% silver content. Depicting detailed reliefs of the twelve Chinese Zodiac animals, this set of silver coins boasts both value as an exclusive collectible in addition to its value as legal tender. The set is limited to a single run of 10,000 worldwide.
