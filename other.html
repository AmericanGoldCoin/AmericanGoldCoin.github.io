2022 Winter Olympics

 This gold coin collection is in commemoration of the future 2022 Winter Olympics.


 In 2015, China successfully bid again for the privilege of hosting the 2022 Winter Olympics in the capital city of Beijing. In order to embody the spirit of the Olympics and celebrate China's rise on the global stage, the American Gold Coin Inc. will be issuing a full set of commemorative gold coins to honor the International Olympics Committee and the Olympic Games as a special limited-edition unique souvenir of the upcoming Winter Olympics.



Chinese Chess

 To honor the long history of Chinese Chess culture, American Gold Coin Inc. is proud to present a set of gold coins featuring a theme of Chinese Chess.


 The collection is inspired by the golden chess set used by Qianlong, Emperor of the Qing Dynasty. It depicts original portraits of Liu Bang, Xiang Yu, and other notable historical figures during the Chu-Han War.



Giant Panda

 This collection consists of a set of 33 coins depicting the Giant Panda.


 As a Chinese National Treasure, Giant Pandas are beloved by the Chinese people and regarded as China's mascots and messenger of world peace. To commemorate the 33rd anniversary of Giant Panda Coins, American Gold Coin Inc. is proud to produce this set of 33 Chinese Panda Gold Coins, with the Giant Panda on the face and the American Bald Eagle logo of American Gold Coin Inc. on the back.



Gods of Fortunes

 To promote traditional Chinese Buddhism culture, American Gold Coin Inc. is happy to present a collection of 12 silver coins showcasing the 9 Gods of Fortunes, including the God of War, Angel of Fortune, and Deities of the Five Directions.


 The coins will also feature brush calligraphy written in the hand of various reknown calligraphers: the Chinese character for good fortune "福" by Mr. Zhao Buchu, the first and former director of China Buddhism Association, the character for Buddhism "佛" by Mage Yicheng, former director of China Buddhism Association, and the blessing "佛光普照行" (meaning "Buddha's glorious light covers all") by Mr. Fu Yinshu, Director of China Buddhism Association. The 12 silver coins are worked in beautiful colored enamels with the image of Buddha.



Rio 2016 Olympic Games

 To celebrate the 2016 Rio Olympics, American Gold Coin Inc. presents a set of collectable coins to commemorate the Rio Olympic Games.



Six Investment Coins

 With the aim of promoting communications and the development of world currency, American Gold Coin Inc. is proud to introduce five investment coins, together with the Chinese Panda coin, to be published internationally.



Dollar Value Chinese Zodiac Coins

 To commemorate the anniversary of the first edition of Chinese Zodiac commemorative coins produced by American Gold Coin Inc. and make Chinese Zodiac Culture more widely accessible, American Gold Coin Inc. has minted a set of dollar-value Chinese Zodiac commemorative coins.


 The whole set consists of 12 coins each pictured with the 12 Zodiac animals, with the face value of 1USD each.



Xu Beihong Chinese Zodiac Gold Coins

 The year 2015 is internationally regarded Chinese artist Xu Beihong's 120th is known not only for his classical Chinese ink paintings of nature, but also to utilize Western art techniques in his depictions of traditional Chinese imagery worked in oils.


 To honor the memory of one of the quintessential Chinese painters of the modern era, American Gold Coin Inc. has created a collection of coins portraying Mr. Xu Beihong's most iconic and influential work on the Chinese Zodiac, cast in 99.9% gold. The Chinese Zodiac condenses Mr. Xu Beihong's lifetime of dedication to his work and his artists' spirit. It is our hope that in recreating the Chinese Zodiac, we will build a bridge between the work of this great artist and us, where we can enjoy the treasure of his work transcending time.
