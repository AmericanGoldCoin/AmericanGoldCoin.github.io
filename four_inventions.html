The Four Great Inventions of Ancient China

 To celebrate the great contributions rendered by the Four Great Inventions of Ancient China to global technology, economics and culture, American Gold Coin Inc. has designed and minted the "Four Great Inventions of Ancient China" collection of commemorative coins. The backs are designed with a relief of an American bald eagle.


 The complete set of 4 coins contain 99.9% silver content and are valued at 10USD.
